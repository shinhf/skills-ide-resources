Write the weekly raise report from the founder's pipeline.

Context:
- If `raise-brief.md` exists in the project root, read it first so priorities are weighted against the founder's stage, ideal investor, and round size.

If the pipeline is missing:
- Ask the founder to paste their pipeline, link a file, or point to a CRM export. Do not invent investor data.

Produce a short report (aim for five tight lines plus the priority list):
1. **By stage** — how many investors are in each stage (e.g. sourced, contacted, replied, meeting, diligence, committed, passed).
2. **Who moved** — investors who advanced a stage this week.
3. **Who went cold** — investors with no reply after the founder's touches; flag the threshold used.
4. **Meetings booked** — calls scheduled or held this week.
5. **Next 3 priorities** — the three highest-leverage actions before the next week.

Keep it to the state of the raise at a glance. No dashboards, no filler.
